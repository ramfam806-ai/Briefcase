Everything you need to know about our templates and purchasing.

How do I get my template after purchasing?

Immediately after payment, you'll be redirected to a download page where you can access your template. We'll also send you a download link via email.

What formats do the templates come in?

Each template type has its own format: Notion templates are shared via a duplicate link, Google Sheets templates are provided as a copy link, and PDF planners come as downloadable PDF files.

No! Our templates are one-time purchases. You pay once and own them forever. No subscriptions, no recurring fees.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Are updates included with my purchase?

Yes! When we improve our templates, you get the updates for free. We'll notify you when new versions are available.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets through Stripe's secure payment platform.
