What is a SMART Goal?

A SMART goal is an objective that meets five specific criteria designed to eliminate ambiguity and focus your attention:

  • S - Specific: Your goal must be clear and defined. (Instead of "I want to grow my business," write "I want to sign 3 new consulting clients.")
  • M - Measurable: You must define exactly how you will measure success. (e.g., "I will generate $15,000 in monthly recurring revenue.")
  • A - Actionable: You must list the specific actions you will take to achieve the goal. (e.g., "I will send 10 cold outreach emails every business day.")
  • R - Relevant: Your goal must align with your broader values and long-term business strategy.
  • T - Time-Bound: You must set a specific deadline. (e.g., "I will achieve this by December 31st.")

Why a Printable Workbook is the Ultimate Goal Execution Tool

A printable workbook PDF is far superior for goal execution because:

  • Physical Visibility: A printed workbook sits on your physical desk. It acts as an un-ignorable, tactile reminder of your daily commitments.
